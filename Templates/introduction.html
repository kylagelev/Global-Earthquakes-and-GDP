What is GDP





Gross domestic product (GDP) is the most commonly used measure for the size of an economy.
GDP can be compiled for a country, a region (such as Tuscany in Italy or Burgundy in France), or for several countries combined, as in the case of the European Union (EU).
The GDP is the total of all value added created in an economy.
