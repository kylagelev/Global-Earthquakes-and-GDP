What Causes an Earthquake







An earthquake is what happens when two blocks of the earth suddenly slip past one another.
The surface where they slip is called the fault or fault plane.
The location below the earth’s surface where the earthquake starts is called the hypocenter,
and the location directly above it on the surface of the earth is called the epicenter.

What is GDP











Gross domestic product (GDP) is the total monetary or market value of all the finished goods and services
produced within a country's borders in a specific time period.
As a broad measure of overall domestic production, it functions as a comprehensive scorecard of a given country’s economic health.
