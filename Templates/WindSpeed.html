Wind Speed

Visualization: City Latitude vs. Wind Speed

Note a slightly positive correlation between Wind Speed and Latitude from Northern Hemisphere. Meaning that the further north, the more likely we can expect a higher wind speed (mph). And that the further south, the more likely we can expect a lower wind speed (mph).
